About Whitestones

Whitestone Properties Management Pvt Ltd is a Bangalore-based real estate company specializing in property advisory and construction services across Bangalore. We collaborate with leading builders such as Sobha, Prestige, and other reputed mid-scale and small-scale developers, offering clients the best deals in residential and commercial spaces. Alongside property advisory, we also undertake construction and project management, ensuring quality, transparency, and timely delivery. Our goal is to help customers find their dream homes, invest smartly, and build lasting assets in Bangalore’s booming real estate market.
